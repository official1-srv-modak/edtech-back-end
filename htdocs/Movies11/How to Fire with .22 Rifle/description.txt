Summary:

The NCC Weapon Training guide on Basic Rules of Firing and How to Fire with a .22 Rifle provides essential instructions for cadets on safely handling and shooting the .22 rifle. It covers fundamental aspects such as the correct stance, aiming techniques, safety measures, and step-by-step firing procedures. This guide is designed to ensure that cadets develop the necessary skills and discipline to fire accurately and responsibly in a controlled environment.
