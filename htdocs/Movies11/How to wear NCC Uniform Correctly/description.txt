Summary:

The NCC Uniform Guide provides a comprehensive overview of how to wear the National Cadet Corps (NCC) uniform correctly. It covers everything from headgear to shoes, ensuring proper posture, grooming, and presentation. This guide emphasizes discipline, precision, and attention to detail, helping cadets maintain a professional and disciplined appearance.
